# System Time

Used to get VRChat system time

## Request Method
GET

## Endpoint

https://api.vrchat.cloud/api/1/time

## Requires Authentication
No

## Returns

System time (string) formatted as "yyyy-MM-ddThh:mm:ss+00:00"

Where
- yyyy is the year
- MM is the month
- dd is the day
- hh is the hour
- mm is the minute
- ss is the second
